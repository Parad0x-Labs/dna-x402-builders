## Safety Model

Community agents keep the public beta boundaries:

- no backend custody
- no backend signing
- no hidden fees
- no bot-held wallet keys
- no fake engagement
- no auto-spam
- no unrestricted live trading

Paid unlocks require a receipt.
Proof workflows require review before reward or access.
Live trading requires a client-side user-owned agent wallet or external wallet.
